Time Out for Dribbble

Time Out is a better Dribbble client for the iPhone, designed for iOS 7. Available now, for free!

Features

Designed for iOS 7

Every decision in Time Out has been made with iOS 7 in mind.

HTML in comments

HTML in comments is properly detected and parsed, so comments will look good and contain no weird HTML tags.

Animated GIF support

Whether you pronounce GIF as 'gif' or 'jif', Time Out displays animated GIFs wonderfully. Oh and by the way, GIF is pronounced 'jif'.

Optimized for iPhone 5s

Time Out has been optimized for iPhone 5s, so you'll enjoy the world's best design 64 bits at a time. Yes.
